 DEMOCRATS  MEDIA 04/892 FRIDAY 10 SEPTEMBER 2004   SENATOR ANDREW BARTLETT LEADER OF THE AUSTRALIAN DEMOCRATS   Fairness the focus for Democrats first fortnight Within the first fortnight of the election campaign the Australian Democrats released a package of Fairness measures including Political Honesty and Accountability, Indigenous issues, Medicare, Refugees, Children and Youth, Poverty and Animal Welfare . Leader Senator Andrew Bartlett said, "The Democrats stand up for fairness. We are proud of our Senate record ensuring that committees inquired into issues, and the public had a say. We did the hard work of reviewing every single piece of legislation and negotiated to improve them."    The Democrats made a difference for those on welfare, sole parents, over 50's job seekers, foster parents, veterans, same sex couples, child migrants, children in care, low income earners, and refugees.
