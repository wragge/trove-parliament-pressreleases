 Media Release 

 62 Sri Lankans Transferred to Christmas Island 

 DPS 10/2002 

 A group of 62 Sri Lankan asylum seekers were transferred from Cocos Island detention facility to Christmas Island detention facility on Saturday, 16 February 2002. 

 Their departure follows the transfer of 180 asylum seekers from Christmas Island to  processing centres on Manus Island and Nauru in late January. 

 These transfers will provide detainees on both Christmas and Cocos Islands with more flexible  living arrangements. 

 The departure of the group means there are 31 detainees remaining on Cocos Island and 88  detainees remaining on Christmas Island.
