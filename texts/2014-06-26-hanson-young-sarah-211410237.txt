 Greens congratulate next Governor of South Australia   

 The Greens have congratulated Vietnamese refugee, Hieu Van Le who is set to become  the next Governor of South Australia.   

 "The appointment of Mr Hieu Van Le as South Australia's next Governor is an important  recognition of the positive contribution that refugees have made to our society", said  Senator Hanson-Young.   

 "I offer my congratulations to Mr Van Le  and his family and wish him all the best in his  new role.   

 "Mr Le has an inspiring life story and should be congratulated on the contribution he has  made to the community of South Australia.   

 "It is great to see South Australia's vibrant, culturally diverse community represented in  leadership positions."
