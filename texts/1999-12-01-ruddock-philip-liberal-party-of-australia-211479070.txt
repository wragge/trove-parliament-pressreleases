  REFUGEE REVIEW TRIBUNAL APPOINTMENT

  

  The Minister for Immigration and Multicultural Affairs, Philip Ruddock, 
has announced the appo intment of Dr Peter Nygh as Acting Principal 
Member of the Refugee Review Tribunal (RRT) for a twelve month period 
until 30 November 2000.

  

 Dr Nygh previously held this position from 3 June 
1998 until 31 May 1999. He agreed to return to the position for a further 
twelve months at the Minister’s request.

  

 Mr Ruddock said that Dr Nygh has considerable stature 
and experience in the legal field and his return to the Tribunal was 
very welcome.

  

 “Dr Nygh was instrumental in improving the performance 
of the RRT during his previous period as Principal Member of the Tribunal,’ 
Mr Ruddock said.

  

 “I am confident that he will ensure that the RRT 
is operating effectively in the lead up to the commencement of the Administrative 
Review Tribunal in February 2001.

  

 “I thank Mr John Godfrey the Deputy Principal Member 
of the RRT for his contribution over the last six months while he was 
acting in the position of Principal Member. His dedication and commitment 
are greatly appreciated,” Mr Ruddock said.
