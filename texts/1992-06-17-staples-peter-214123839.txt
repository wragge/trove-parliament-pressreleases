 Presentation of Paul Cullen Award for contribution to  refugee well-being

 Wednesday June 17  10am Committee Room 2S3, Parliament House

 The Minister for Justice and Minister Assisting the Minister for Immigration, Senator  Michael Tate, will present the 1992 Paul Cullen Award at Parliament House tomorrow  (Wednesday), to coincide with the national launch of Refugee Week.

 The Paul Cullen Award is sponsored by AUSTCARE, and perpetuates the name of Major-  General Paul Cullen in recognition of his outstanding work for refugees since 1936 as  AUSTCAREâ€™s first President, and as inaugural President of the Refugee Council of  Australia.

 The aim of the award is to recognise outstanding contribnutions to the well-being of refugees  and to promote awareness in the Australian community of refugee needs. The award winner  has been selected from public nominations of persons working in the refugee field.
