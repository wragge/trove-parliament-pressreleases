  Suspected Illegal Vessels - update

  

  Minister for Justice and Customs, Senator Amanda Vanstone, announced 
today  that another vessel carrying suspected illegal immigrants 
had arrived at Ashmore Reef off Western Australia’s north, after being 
intercepted by Customs.

  

 The vessel was intercepted by the Customs vessel, 
Botany Bay, while it was monitoring another vessel 40 kilometres north-west 
of the Reef.

  

 The Botany Bay has accompanied the vessel, carrying 
24 suspected illegal entrants plus two crew into Ashmore Reef.

  

 The HMAS Whyalla has now assumed monitoring of the 
other vessel, believed to be carrying 24 people plus 5 crew, east of 
Ashmore Reef, still outside Australian territorial waters.

  

 Two boats arrived another at Ash Officials from yesterday, 
one at Christmas Island carrying a total of 62 people and more: Reef 
carrying 33 suspected illegal entrants plus two crew.

  

 Immigration will process the latest arrivals in the 
normal way.
